# 03-sentence-bank


## ### 总结前人研究

**中文：**
- [作者]等([年份])对[对象]进行了研究，发现[结论]。
- 众多学者对[问题]开展了大量研究，取得了丰硕成果。
- 前人研究主要集中在以下几个方面：一是[方面1]，二是[方面2]，三是[方面3]。
- 综合已有研究可以发现，[总结性发现]。

**English:**
- [Author] et al. ([year]) investigated [subject] and found that [finding].
- Extensive studies have been conducted on [topic], yielding significant results.
- Previous research has primarily focused on [aspect 1], [aspect 2], and [aspect 3].
- A synthesis of existing literature reveals that [summary finding].

### 引用具体文献

**中文：**
- [作者]([年份])指出，[观点/结论]。
- 据[作者]([年份])报道，[发现]。
- [作者]([年份])的研究表明，[结论]。
- [作者]([年份])采用[方法]对[对象]进行了分析，结果显示[结果]。

**English:**
- [Author] ([year]) demonstrated that [finding].
- It has been reported that [finding] ([Author], [year]).
- According to [Author] ([year), [statement].
- Using [method], [Author] ([year]) analyzed [subject] and reported that [result].

### 对比文献结论

**中文：**
- 与[研究1]不同，[研究2]发现[不同结论]，这可能与[原因]有关。
- [研究1]和[研究2]得出了相似/相悖的结论。
- 然而，[作者]([年份])在[不同条件]下的研究得出了不同结论。
- 上述研究在[方面]取得了重要进展，但在[方面]仍存在不足。

**English:**
- In contrast to [study 1], [study 2] reported [different finding], which may be attributed to [reason].
- [Study 1] and [study 2] yielded similar/contradictory conclusions.
- However, [Author] ([year]) reached a different conclusion under [different conditions].
- While significant progress has been made in [aspect], gaps remain in [aspect].

## 三、研究空白与问题提出

### 指出研究空白

**中文：**
- 目前，关于[问题]的研究仍相对较少/尚不充分。
- 尽管[已有研究]，但[具体空白]仍缺乏系统研究。
- 现有研究多集中于[方面A]，而对[方面B]关注不足。
- 已有研究多采用[方法A]，而[方法B]的应用尚不普遍。
- 上述研究为本课题提供了重要参考，但[不足]仍需进一步探讨。

**English:**
- To date, limited studies have focused on [topic].
- Despite [existing research], [specific gap] has not been systematically investigated.
- Most previous studies have focused on [aspect A], while [aspect B] remains underexplored.
- While [method A] has been widely adopted, the application of [method B] is still limited.
- The aforementioned studies provide valuable references; however, [gap] requires further investigation.

### 本研究的目的

**中文：**
- 本研究旨在[目的]，以期为[应用]提供科学依据。
- 为此，本研究以[对象]为研究对象，采用[方法]，对[内容]进行系统分析。
- 本研究的主要目标是：(1) [目标1]；(2) [目标2]；(3) [目标3]。
- 基于此，本研究拟从[角度]出发，探讨[问题]。

**English:**
- This study aims to [objective], providing a scientific basis for [application].
- To this end, [subject] was investigated using [methods] to systematically analyze [content].
- The primary objectives of this study are: (1) [objective 1]; (2) [objective 2]; (3) [objective 3].
- Accordingly, this study examines [topic] from the perspective of [angle].

## 十、过渡与连接词

### 段落过渡

**中文：**
- 在此基础上，进一步分析了[内容]。
- 为了深入理解[问题]，本研究还[做了什么]。
- 基于上述分析，接下来将讨论[内容]。
- 除了[方面]，[另一方面]同样值得关注。

**English:**
- Building on this, [content] was further analyzed.
- To gain deeper insight into [issue], this study also [did what].
- Based on the above analysis, [content] will be discussed next.
- In addition to [aspect], [another aspect] also deserves attention.

### 逻辑连接

**中文：**
- 因此/据此/基于此：用于推导结论
- 然而/但是/相反：用于转折
- 此外/另外/同时：用于补充
- 具体而言/换句话说/也就是说：用于解释
- 特别是/尤其是：用于强调
- 尽管/虽然：用于让步

**English:**
- Therefore / Accordingly / Thus: for drawing conclusions
- However / Nevertheless / In contrast: for transitions
- Moreover / Furthermore / Additionally: for additions
- Specifically / In other words / That is: for explanations
- Particularly / Especially: for emphasis
- Although / Despite / While: for concessions

## 十一、环境科学领域特有句式

### 污染物描述

**中文：**
- [污染物]是[来源]产生的主要[类型]污染物之一。
- [污染物]的[浓度/含量]远超/低于[标准值]。
- [区域]的[污染水平]处于[评价]水平。
- [污染物]在[相态]中的赋存形态主要包括[形态1]和[形态2]。

**English:**
- [Pollutant] is one of the primary [type] pollutants originating from [source].
- The [concentration/content] of [pollutant] far exceeded / was well below [standard value].
- The [pollution level] in [area] was classified as [assessment] level.
- The speciation of [pollutant] in [phase] primarily includes [form 1] and [form 2].

### 碳循环相关

**中文：**
- 碳平衡分析表明，[系统]中碳的[输入/输出]主要通过[途径]实现。
- [系统]的碳通量为[X]，其中[途径1]占[Y]%，[途径2]占[Z]%。
- 固、液、气三相态碳的分配比例分别为[X:Y:Z]。
- [系统]整体呈碳[汇/源]特征。

**English:**
- Carbon balance analysis indicated that carbon [input/output] in [system] was primarily achieved through [pathway].
- The carbon flux of [system] was [X], of which [pathway 1] accounted for [Y]% and [pathway 2] accounted for [Z]%.
- The distribution ratios of carbon in solid, liquid, and gas phases were [X:Y:Z], respectively.
- [System] exhibited an overall carbon [sink/source] characteristic.
