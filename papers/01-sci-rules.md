# SCI写作规则库

## 一、核心原则

### SCI论文的本质
- **目标：** 向同行传递新知识
- **标准：** 可重复性（reproducibility）+ 可信性（credibility）
- **语言：** 简洁、准确、客观

### 写作三不原则
1. **不夸大：** 用 suggest/indicate/demonstrate，不用 prove/confirm
2. **不模糊：** 具体数据 > 形容词（significant > very large）
3. **不冗余：** 每句话必须有信息量

---

## 二、时态规则

这是SCI写作中最容易出错的部分。

### 各部分时态速查表

| 部分 | 主要时态 | 例外 |
|------|---------|------|
| Abstract - Background | **现在时** | 已完成的具体研究用过去时 |
| Abstract - Methods | **过去时** | - |
| Abstract - Results | **过去时** | - |
| Abstract - Conclusions | **现在时** | - |
| Introduction | **现在时** | 具体文献报道用过去时 |
| Materials & Methods | **过去时** | 引用标准/通用方法可用现在时 |
| Results | **过去时** | - |
| Discussion - 本研究发现 | **过去时** | - |
| Discussion - 文献对比 | **现在时** | 具体研究结果用过去时 |
| Discussion - 机制解释 | **现在时** | - |
| Conclusions | **现在时** | - |

### 时态例句

**Introduction - 现在时（通用知识/当前状态）：**
```
Carbon pollutants in sewage systems pose significant environmental challenges.
Previous studies have focused primarily on liquid-phase carbon.
However, the multi-phase distribution of carbon remains poorly understood.
```

**Introduction - 过去时（具体文献）：**
```
Zhang et al. (2023) reported that TOC concentrations ranged from 10 to 200 mg/L.
Li and Wang (2022) investigated carbon cycling in urban drainage systems.
```

**Methods - 过去时：**
```
Samples were collected at 12 sampling sites during winter and spring.
TOC was determined using a total organic carbon analyzer.
Data were analyzed using SPSS 26.0.
```

**Results - 过去时：**
```
TOC concentrations ranged from 12.3 to 156.8 mg/L.
The difference between winter and spring was significant (p = 0.002).
PCA revealed two principal components explaining 71.0% of the total variance.
```

**Discussion - 混合时态：**
```
本研究发现（过去时）：
We found that TOC in winter was significantly higher than in spring.

文献对比（现在时）：
This finding is consistent with the results of Smith et al. (2022).

机制解释（现在时）：
Low temperatures reduce microbial decomposition rates, leading to carbon accumulation.
```

**Conclusions - 现在时：**
```
This study provides new insights into carbon pollutant distribution.
The results indicate that functional zone type is the dominant factor.
```

## 三、语态规则

### 被动语态（Methods中的默认语态）

**为什么用被动：** 强调动作和对象，而非执行者

**常用结构：**
```
Samples were collected/analyzed/determined using...
Data were processed/analyzed by...
Statistical analyses were performed using...
```

**注意：** 不要写 "We collected samples" 而要写 "Samples were collected"

### 主动语态（在某些部分可接受）

**可接受场景：**
- Discussion中表达观点："We suggest that..."
- Conclusion中总结："This study demonstrates that..."
- 但被动仍然更常见

## 四、各部分写作规范

### Title（标题）

**规则：**
- 10-15个实词（不含a/the/of/in等虚词）
- 避免缩写（除非如TOC、COD、PCA等公认缩写）
- 不用问句
- 不用"Study on..."或"Investigation of..."开头（老套）
- 包含关键变量和研究范围

**好标题 vs 差标题：**
```
差：A Study on Carbon Pollutants in Sewage Systems
好：Multi-Phase Distribution of Carbon Pollutants in Campus Sewage Networks

差：The Analysis of TOC in Different Areas
好：Spatial and Seasonal Variations of TOC in Urban Drainage Systems

差：Carbon Balance
好：Carbon Balance in Solid-Liquid-Gas Phases in Campus Sewage Networks
```

**标题结构模板：**
```
[关键特征/方法] of [研究对象] in [研究范围]
```

**本课题标题建议：**
```
Distribution and Balance of Carbon Pollutants across
Solid-Liquid-Gas Phases in Campus Sewage Networks
```

---

### Abstract（摘要）

**结构：Background(1) + Objective(1) + Methods(1-2) + Results(2-3) + Conclusions(1)**
**字数：** 200-300 words（期刊要求不同）
**时态：** Background/Conclusions 现在时；Methods/Results 过去时

**模板：**
```
[Background] Carbon pollutant distribution in multi-phase sewage systems
is critical for accurate carbon accounting, yet remains poorly understood
in campus sewage networks.

[Objective] This study investigated the occurrence and distribution of
carbon pollutants in solid, liquid, and gas phases in a campus sewage
network, and conducted a carbon balance analysis.

[Methods] Samples were collected from [n] sampling sites during winter
and spring. Solid-phase, liquid-phase, and gas-phase carbon indicators
were determined using [methods]. Multivariate statistical analyses
including PCA and HCA were employed.

[Results] TOC concentrations ranged from [X] to [Y] mg/L, with
significantly higher values in winter than in spring (p < 0.05).
PCA identified [n] principal components explaining [X]% of the total
variance. The carbon balance revealed that [finding].

[Conclusions] This study provides new insights into multi-phase carbon
dynamics in campus sewage networks, with implications for urban carbon
management.
```

**常见错误：**
- Abstract中出现图表引用（"as shown in Figure 1"）
- 方法描述过于详细
- 结果缺乏具体数据
- 使用缩写未定义

---

### Introduction（引言）

**结构：漏斗模型（4段式）**

**Paragraph 1 - 大背景（3-5句）：**
```
[大背景句] [领域重要性] [具体问题的引出]
```
- 从全球/国家层面切入
- 逐步聚焦到具体领域
- 引出核心问题

**Paragraph 2 - 文献综述（5-8句）：**
```
[前人做了什么] [主要发现] [使用的典型方法]
```
- 按时间/主题组织
- 不是简单罗列，要有批判性
- 突出研究趋势

**Paragraph 3 - 研究空白（3-5句）：**
```
Despite these efforts, [空白1] remains unclear.
Moreover, [空白2] has received limited attention.
In particular, [空白3] needs further investigation.
```
- 用"However/Despite/Although"引出空白
- 空白要具体，不要说"很少有人研究"
- 空白要与本研究直接相关

**Paragraph 4 - 本研究（3-5句）：**
```
Therefore, this study aims to [目标].
Specifically, [具体目标1]; [具体目标2]; [具体目标3].
To achieve these objectives, [简述方法].
The results will provide [预期贡献].
```

**Introduction常用信号词：**
- 引出背景：Recently, In recent decades, With the increasing...
- 引出文献：Several studies have shown that..., Previous research has demonstrated...
- 引出空白：However, ..., remains unclear/limited/poorly understood
- 引出目的：Therefore, the aims of this study were to...

---

### Materials and Methods（材料与方法）

**时态：** 全部过去时
**语态：** 被动语态为主
**原则：** 详细到可重复

**应包含的信息：**
1. 研究区域概况（位置、气候、特征）
2. 采样方案（时间、地点、方法、数量）
3. 分析方法（仪器型号、参数、标准）
4. 数据处理（软件、统计方法）
5. 质量控制（平行样、空白样、回收率）

**模板：**
```
2.1 Study area
[Location], [climate], [sewage system characteristics].

2.2 Sample collection
Samples were collected at [n] sites during [period].
At each site, [replicates] were collected.
[保存方法]. Samples were transported to the laboratory under [条件].

2.3 Analytical methods
[Parameter A] was determined using [instrument] ([manufacturer], [model])
according to [standard]. [Parameter B] was measured by [method].
详细参数设置...

2.4 Statistical analysis
Data were analyzed using [software] ([version]).
[具体统计方法].
A p-value < 0.05 was considered statistically significant.

2.5 Quality assurance and quality control
[QA/QC措施].
Method blanks, duplicate samples, and standard reference materials
were used for quality control. Recovery rates ranged from [X]% to [Y]%.
```

**常见错误：**
- 缺少设备型号和参数
- 未说明质量控制措施
- 统计方法未说明选择依据
- 缺少数据预处理步骤（如异常值剔除）

---

### Results（结果）

**时态：** 过去时
**原则：** 客观描述，不做推断（推断留给Discussion）

**写作模式：** 先总体后具体，先文字后图表

**组织方式选择：**
- 按指标组织：TOC → DOC → POC → CH₄ → CO₂
- 按主题组织：空间分布 → 时间变化 → 相关性 → PCA
- 按相态组织：固相 → 液相 → 气相 → 多相态关联

**单个结果描述模板：**
```
[指标] [变化趋势] across [分组] (Fig. [X]).
The values ranged from [min] to [max], with a mean of [mean]±[SD] (Table [X]).
[最高点] showed the highest [value], while [最低点] showed the lowest [value].
[差异检验] revealed significant differences among [groups] ([statistic], p = [value]).
```

**图表引用规则：**
- 先在正文中描述结果，然后说"(Fig. X)"或"(Table X)"
- 不要说"as shown in Fig. X"（这个在Discussion中可以用）
- Results中图表应自明（self-explanatory）

---

### Discussion（讨论）

**时态混合：**
- 本研究发现：过去时
- 文献对比：现在时（一般性）或过去时（具体结果）
- 机制解释：现在时
- 局限性/展望：现在时

**结构参照：** [[04-discussion-library.md]]

---

### Conclusions（结论）

**时态：** 现在时
**字数：** 150-200 words（或3-5个要点）

**模板：**
```
This study systematically investigated [content] in [subject].
The main findings are as follows:
(1) [Finding 1].
(2) [Finding 2].
(3) [Finding 3].
These results provide [contribution] and have implications for [application].
```

**注意：**
- 不要引入新数据
- 不要重复Abstract
- 每条结论要对应Results中的具体发现

## 五、语言规范

### 禁用词/表达

| 禁用 | 替代 | 原因 |
|------|------|------|
| prove | demonstrate/suggest/indicate | 科学研究不能"证明" |
| very | extremely/remarkably 或直接删掉 | 空洞的程度副词 |
| a lot of | considerable/substantial | 不够正式 |
| get | obtain/achieve | 口语化 |
| big/large | significant/substantial | 不够精确 |
| show | reveal/demonstrate/indicate | 更精确 |
| find out | discover/reveal | 更正式 |
| think | suggest/believe/consider | 更学术 |
| good | favorable/appropriate/positive | 模糊 |
| bad | unfavorable/adverse/negative | 模糊 |

### 推荐学术动词

**描述发现：**
- reveal, demonstrate, indicate, suggest, show
- confirm, support, validate
- observe, detect, identify, determine

**表达差异：**
- differ, vary, range
- exceed, surpass
- approach, approximate

**表达关系：**
- correlate, associate, relate
- contribute, attribute
- derive, originate

**表达变化：**
- increase, decrease, fluctuate
- enhance, reduce, diminish
- peak, decline, stabilize

### 缩写规则
- 首次使用写全称，括号内注明缩写：total organic carbon (TOC)
- 之后统一使用缩写
- 标题和摘要中首次出现也需要定义
- 公认缩写可直接使用：DNA, pH, km, etc.

## 六、各期刊风格差异

### 环境科学领域主要期刊

| 期刊 | 影响因子 | 特点 | 投稿偏好 |
|------|---------|------|---------|
| Environmental Science & Technology | ~11 | 领域顶刊 | 创新性+应用性 |
| Water Research | ~12 | 水领域顶刊 | 系统性+深度 |
| Science of the Total Environment | ~9 | 综合性环境 | 广度+数据量 |
| Environmental Pollution | ~8 | 污染方向 | 新颖性 |
| Chemosphere | ~8 | 化学+环境 | 机理研究 |
| Journal of Environmental Sciences | ~6 | 国际化 | 研究完整性 |

### 不同类型论文的要求

**研究型论文（Research Article）：**
- 标准IMRaD结构
- 3000-6000 words
- 5-8个图/表

**短通讯（Short Communication）：**
- 紧凑结构
- 2000-3000 words
- 2-4个图/表

**综述（Review）：**
- 全面的文献覆盖
- 6000-10000 words
- 需要系统的文献搜索策略

## 七、SCI投稿前检查清单

### 格式检查
- [ ] 符合目标期刊的Author Guidelines
- [ ] 字数在规定范围内
- [ ] 图/表数量符合要求
- [ ] 参考文献格式正确
- [ ] 补充材料完整

### 内容检查
- [ ] Title简洁且包含关键信息
- [ ] Abstract包含背景、方法、结果、结论
- [ ] Introduction逻辑清晰（背景→文献→空白→目的）
- [ ] Methods详细到可重复
- [ ] Results客观描述，无推断
- [ ] Discussion解释了"为什么"，不仅重复"是什么"
- [ ] Conclusions与Results对应

### 语言检查
- [ ] 各部分时态正确
- [ ] 被动语态在Methods中使用
- [ ] 无语法错误
- [ ] 无拼写错误
- [ ] 术语使用一致
- [ ] 缩写首次使用已定义

### 图表检查
- [ ] 分辨率 ≥ 300 dpi
- [ ] 字体大小可读（≥ 8pt）
- [ ] 颜色可区分（考虑色盲读者）
- [ ] 图表标题完整
- [ ] 图表在正文中已引用

### 伦理检查
- [ ] 所有作者同意投稿
- [ ] 无一稿多投
- [ ] 数据真实可靠
- [ ] 致谢已获同意
- [ ] 利益冲突声明

### 投稿材料检查
- [ ] Cover Letter
- [ ] Title Page（含作者信息、通讯作者、ORCID）
- [ ] Main Manuscript（双倍行距/符合格式）
- [ ] Figures（单独文件或嵌入）
- [ ] Tables（单独文件或嵌入）
- [ ] Supplementary Materials
- [ ] Highlights（3-5条，每条85字符以内）
- [ ] Graphical Abstract（如需要）
- [ ] Credit Author Statement
- [ ] Declaration of Interest

## 八、环境科学领域高频表达

### 开头句式
```
Carbon cycling in [systems] plays a crucial role in [context].
Sewage networks serve as important pathways for [process].
Multi-phase distribution of [pollutants] has attracted increasing attention.
```

### 数据描述
```
Concentrations of [X] ranged from [min] to [max] [units].
A significant [positive/negative] correlation was observed between [A] and [B] (r = [value], p < [value]).
[Parameter] exhibited a [increasing/decreasing] trend from [A] to [B].
```

### 对比文献
```
Our findings are in good agreement with [Author] ([year]), who reported [finding].
In contrast to [Author] ([year]), we observed [different finding].
The values obtained in this study were [higher/lower/comparable] to those reported in [reference].
```

### 机制解释
```
This can be attributed to [mechanism].
The underlying mechanism involves [process].
[Factor] may account for the observed [pattern].
```

### 环境意义
```
These findings have important implications for [application].
The results provide a scientific basis for [management/policy].
Understanding [process] is essential for [environmental goal].
```
